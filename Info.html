PURPOSE OF AIRLINES

Air India is the largest international carrier out of India with an 18.6% market share. Over 60 international destinations are served by Air India across five continents. The airline became the 27th member of Star Alliance on 11 July 2014. The airline was founded by J. R. D. An airline is a company that provides air transport services for traveling passengers and/or freight. Airlines use aircraft to supply these services and may form partnerships or alliances with other airlines for codeshare agreements, in which they both offer and operate the same flight. Generally, airline companies are recognized with an air operating certificate or license issued by a governmental aviation body. Airlines may be scheduled or charter operators

ADVANTAGES OF AIRLINE

  1. Air transport is one of the fastest modes of travel.
  2. Aeroplanes can carry people, mail and lightweight goods to distant places in the shortest time possible.
  3. They also prove to be extremely useful during the times of natural calamities, wars and other emergencies.
